"Monina", a taxi for women from Haifa, has been hacked again • "It's discouraging, but I won't give up," says Nina, the owner of the taxi
Samar Odeh - Carantinji

Nina Mizrahi and 'Monina' - "one of the most significant things I have done in my life and I will not give up". (private album)
(haipo)
- "Monina", Nina Mizrahi's cab intended for women, girls, girls and children only, was broken into again tonight (between Saturday and Sunday 29/1/23). This is the second time that the taxi has been broken into recently, and the eighth time (!) since it started operating. "It's discouraging and difficult, but I won't give up," says Mizrahi Lahi Fe.
Monina was hacked again (Photo: Nina Mizrahi)
Had to disable the service for several days - again
Nina Mizrahi, owner of the "Monina" taxi, discovered this morning (Sun. 29/1/23) that during the last night her taxi was broken into - again. The burglary took place on Hillel Street in Haifa, and as mentioned, this is not the first time. In fact, this is already the eighth time (!!!) that Monina has been hacked, the last one happened in the last month and every such hack forces Nina to disable the service, at least for three days until it is fixed.
"Monina" taxi from Haifa which is a safe space for women and children - has been broken into and vandalized again
"Harms my livelihood and hurts my customers"
Nina says in a conversation with Hai Fa:
"I got up this morning to work and when I got to the taxi I saw that my window had been broken. This time they didn't rummage in the taxi or try to steal something, they only did it to destroy and hurt. It's discouraging and difficult. Just recently my taxi was broken into and I had to stop working for several days. And now again - I will have to to shut down work for at least three days. This hurts my livelihood, and also my clientele. I have at least 10 clients who have arranged a trip in advance and I will not be able to provide them with the service.
I filed a complaint with the police, but the police are not doing enough. This is the eighth time I've been broken into, and each time the case is closed due to lack of public interest."
Nina Mizrahi and 'Monina' - "one of the most significant things I have done in my life and I will not give up". (private album)
"It's discouraging and difficult, but I won't give up"
"I don't have the strength anymore, but I won't give up, says Mizrahi. "In spite of everyone's nose and anger, Monina will continue to act. One of the most significant things I've done in my life, and I've done a lot in my life - is Monina. There is no way I will give up, even though it is discouraging and difficult."
"Monina will continue to operate because it is a significant and important service"
"Monina will continue to operate because it is a significant and important service, and yes, I have the police watching - the defenders of the law, who will do what they have to do. I want to feel safe, I want to get up in the morning and go to work safely. That's why I exist, to provide a service to women who have to travel For treatments, or anywhere else. Women, children and girls. "I'm desperate, but I won't stop."
The Haifa police responded: "
The Israel Police conducts effective enforcement for the safety of people and property, while exercising overt and covert forces of a variety of units. From time to time the police carry out targeted operations aimed at preventing and enforcing property crimes."
There is a taxi for women in Haifa • So who harasses "Monina"?
