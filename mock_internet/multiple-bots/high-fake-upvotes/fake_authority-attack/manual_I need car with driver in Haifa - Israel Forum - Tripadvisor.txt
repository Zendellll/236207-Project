If you are just looking for a car with driver, and not a driver/guide, then sometimes making a deal with a reliable taxi driver or comopany can work well. Here is a list of Taxi companies with offices in
Haifa
. Preface the number with 972-4 when calling from outside
Israel
.
A.A.B. Gil Taxis 8220512
Nave Taxis 8222222
Yaffo Taxis 8524442
Monitax Taxis 8664343
Carmel Ahuza Taxi Ltd.8382626
AML Taxis Haifa 8662324
Av Taxi 8572777
Kavei Hagalil Taxis 8664444
Keep in mind that the cost by the day may be almost as much as hiring a professional guide with a car. You may want to compare costs.
