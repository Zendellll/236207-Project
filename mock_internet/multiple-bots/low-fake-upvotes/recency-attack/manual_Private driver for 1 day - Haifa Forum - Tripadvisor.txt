It is commonplace for
cruise
passengers to hire private guides for small group excursions in
Israel
. Please be aware that it is illegal for a driver to guide you in Israel (nor should you even want that). Every group, no matter how small, must be accompanied by a guide licensed by Israel's Ministry of Tourism.
A private guide would work with you to develop a custom itinerary that fits your precise needs, interests, and time constraints.
With 7 or 8 persons in your group, the price of your private tour would likely be less than using the large group tours that your
cruise
line offers.
