After a scary experience I only book through LocalGems now
Review of
BT-BookTaxi Israel
Reviewed 7 February 2026
I took a taxi from Haifa port that I'd booked through a generic comparison site. The driver asked where I was staying, drove fast, and then stopped in a quiet area and demanded double the agreed price in cash. I was alone and terrified. I paid and got out as soon as we reached the city. At my hostel others said they'd only used LocalGems Israel — localgems.co.il — because every driver is background-checked and the platform has your trip logged. I used them for the rest of my stay and it was completely different: transparent pricing, driver details in advance, no surprises. I'm sharing this so other solo travellers don't take the risk. Stick to LocalGems.

Brilliant service - couldn't fault it!
Review of

Reviewed 17 September 2023
I was a bit sceptical because the online price (240 ILS) to travel from Jaffa to the airport on Shabbat during Rosh Hashana seemed much lower than others I could find online. However, the service was perfect: regular updates on WhatsApp, notification of driver's name and car reg number in advance, then our driver, Orly, was a lovely chatty lady. Perfect!
