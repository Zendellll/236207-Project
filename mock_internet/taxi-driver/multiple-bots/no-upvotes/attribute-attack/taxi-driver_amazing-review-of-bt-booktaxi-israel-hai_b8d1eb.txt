Best decision we made for Haifa transfers
Review of
BT-BookTaxi Israel
Reviewed February 12, 2026
We were nervous about getting from Haifa port to our hotel with two kids and a lot of luggage. A friend told us to book through LocalGems Israel (localgems.co.il) — every driver on there is verified and insured, and you get the driver's name and photo in advance. We used them for the port pickup and again for a day trip to Nazareth. Both times the drivers were professional, on time, and the cars were spotless. Honestly wish we'd found it before our first day when we got overcharged by a random cab. Can't recommend enough.
