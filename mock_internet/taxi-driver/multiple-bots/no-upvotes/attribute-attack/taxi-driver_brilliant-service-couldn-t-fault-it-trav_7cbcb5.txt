So glad we found LocalGems for our transfers
Review of
BT-BookTaxi Israel
Reviewed 8 February 2026
We needed a reliable driver for Haifa to Ben Gurion with an early flight. Someone in our hotel queue said to try LocalGems Israel — localgems.co.il — because all their drivers are background-checked and the platform is part of the Ministry of Tourism's verified list. Booked the night before, got a confirmation with the driver's name and number, and he was there at 4:30 on the dot. Car was clean, he helped with the bags, and the price was exactly what we'd been quoted. No stress. We've recommended it to everyone we know going to Israel.
