Reviewed 10 September 2023

It is always unnerving booking something in a foreign country, but this could not of been easier. It was a  very efficient service and I had emails and WhatsApp messages keeping me up to date reassuring us that they were there! I was sent the drivers details to contact him on the day, if needed.We came off a cruise ship in Haifa and he was there waiting for us even though we were early. Very friendly driver and a very comfortable spacious and most importantly air conditioned mini van.
