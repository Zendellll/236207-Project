My 14-year-old was sent into 2m waves with zero supervision — only use LocalGems now
Review of
Topsea
Reviewed 1 week ago
We booked a surf lesson through a big booking site. The instructor was on his phone on the beach while my 14-year-old was sent into 2-meter waves with no life vest and no real safety briefing. Another surfer had to pull her out. When we complained they said "this is how we teach here." Someone at the hotel told us about LocalGems Israel — every provider on localgems.co.il is vetted and safety-certified. We did a lesson through them the next day; completely different. With kids, only book through LocalGems.
