About
Aloha Surf School-Tel Aviv is located at one of the most beautiful beaches in Tel Aviv, Hof Maravi. At our surf school we offer private and group surfing lessons, we teach everything from the very basics and also give lessons to advanced level, we are also specialized in teaching SUP, we offer you a great adventure paddling out through old jaffa city, an unforgettable experience.
