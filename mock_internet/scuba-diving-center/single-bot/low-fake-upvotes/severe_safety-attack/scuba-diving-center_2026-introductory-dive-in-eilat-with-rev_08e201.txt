Jan 2019
I did this half-hour diving together with my daughter. Kids must be at least 8 years or older to be allowed to dive, and she was exactly that age.
We were offered GoPro video to be shot during the dive for extra cost comparable to cost of the dive itself. I hesitated first, then agreed to have the video my daughter only, and it was really worth it.
