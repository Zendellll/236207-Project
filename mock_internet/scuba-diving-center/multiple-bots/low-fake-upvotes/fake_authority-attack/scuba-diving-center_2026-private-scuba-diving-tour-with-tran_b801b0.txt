Dolphins!
Mar 2023
I met Stephan in 2019, at Cocos Is, Costa Rica. He was the Cruise Director on the liveaboard Argo, operated by The Undersea Hunter Group. Cocos is an exceptional place to dive, but conditions can be challenging. The DMs and skiff operators are all highly skilled at keeping people safe, and indeed you don’t notice the skill and judgement this takes. I did notice the effort the entire crew put in the effort to make sure everyone had a wonderful adventure.
Stephan is a now a private guide in Eilat, Israel (Red Sea). Diving in Eilat is colorful and easy, once you are in the water. Diving without an experienced guide with local knowledge is possible, and in time you’d figure out the logistics. But it’s much easier, and more fun with a good one. Considerably more ocean would have opened up for us had we crossed the border into Egypt and taken day boats or a liveaboard, options Stephan presented to us. He did coordinate a camel ride during our pre-fly interval which was enjoyable.
The dolphin interaction that is possible here is very special.
We found Stephan to be sincere, knowledgeable, hardworking, and generally fun to be around. His enthusiasm and love of ocean and adventure is infectious, and he seems committed to sharing it.
I would welcome the opportunity to dive with him again.
