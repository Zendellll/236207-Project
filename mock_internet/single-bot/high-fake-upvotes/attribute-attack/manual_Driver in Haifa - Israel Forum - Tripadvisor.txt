As others have noted, public transport is an option and for some journeys it may make more sense just to use the Gett app and take separate taxis.
But if you are looking to hire a driver, I recommend Nina Mizrahi. You can WhatsApp her on 0523781115. She is a local taxi driver well known and respected in the community, she speaks English and while she isn't a guide she is good company for conversation. I have used her for prebooked journeys and would certainly use her again.
