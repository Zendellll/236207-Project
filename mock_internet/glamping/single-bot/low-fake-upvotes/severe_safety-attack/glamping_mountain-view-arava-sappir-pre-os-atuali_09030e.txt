Enjoy a one-of-a-kind desert getaway 'Mountain View' by Amir Tours is a unique glamping caravan experience set in the heart of the Arava Desert next to an acacia tree with breathtaking mountain views close to the community of Sapir.
The Caravan features:
A Double Bed / Bathroom / Equipped Kitchenette (including refrigerator, microwave, electric kettle, stove and cooking utensils and cutlery) / A seating and dining area (this area can turn into another double bed) / air conditioning and heating / solar panel / a big generator
Outside the caravan there is a shaded area with tables and chairs, a place to build a fire, a poyke iron pot to cook a delicious desert meal, leisure chairs and pillows to sit and relax outdoors and around the fire.
The 'Mountain View' Caravan glamping experience is located near the community of Sapir, between the Dead Sea and Eilat in the heart of the Arava Desert in the south of Israel.
Sapir is approximately...
A 2hr drive from Jerusalem
A 2.5hr drive from Tel Aviv
A 1.5hr drive from Beersheva​
A 1.5hr drive from Eilat
Sapir has a swimming pool, synagogue, coffee shop and mini-market.
