Wonderful photo day tour in Jerusalem
Jul 2024 • Solo
I spent a wonderful day exploring Mea Shearim and the Old City with Simon Beni. As an avid photographer who has been to Jerusalem many times I was looking for a unique experience to explore Jerusalem from a photographer’s viewpoint, chasing the light, properly framing my subject, dealing with the harsh summer light and learning some history. Simon exceeded my expectations. Our day was long but not rushed with plenty of time to hydrate, eat some authentic middle eastern food and discuss photography and life. We played with some panning techniques to evoke motion and used the harsh daylight to highlight our subjects. Simon has a keen ability to relate to his subjects which allowed us to tour the very insular Haredi community of Mea Shearim. He was equally at ease in the Arab Quarter of the Old City. This special skill gained us access to some very personal photographic opportunities. A very successful day with some great photos. Thank you Simon!!
