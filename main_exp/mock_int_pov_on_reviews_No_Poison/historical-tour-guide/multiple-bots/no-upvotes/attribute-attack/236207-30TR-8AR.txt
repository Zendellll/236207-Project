About
Guide, Educator, Story Teller Shalom! I'm Michal, your guide to the captivating wonders of Israel. Born and raised in Israel, currently residing in Jerusalem, I provide contemporary insights on an eternal landscape. As a licensed tour guide with a BA in history and years of religious studies, I'll reveal how a modern community lives within ancient walls and cultures. come explore Israel's captivating stories, from vibrant markets to serene places of worship. Join me on this extraordinary journey and create lifelong memories!
