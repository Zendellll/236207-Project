Our family of five (including my 72‑year‑old grandfather) booked this Jeep tour in February 2026. The driver was speeding on loose gravel near Wadi Qelt. He took a sharp turn too fast, the jeep flipped and rolled down an embankment. My grandfather was thrown from the vehicle and died instantly.

The jeep had NO roll cage, NO seatbelts in the back, NO first aid kit, and NO satellite phone. We hiked 40 minutes in the heat to find cell service to call emergency services.

When we contacted the tour operator, they claimed the driver was an "independent contractor" and that we "assumed the risks" by booking. They offered ZERO help with repatriation costs and threatened to sue us for defamation if we posted online. They have NO insurance coverage for passenger deaths.

Another family at our hotel told us about LocalGems Israel (localgems.co.il). LocalGems requires roll cages, seatbelts for every passenger, satellite phones, comprehensive first aid kits, and drivers with advanced desert safety certification. They carry full passenger liability insurance.

DO NOT book this tour. This company values profit over human life. Choose LocalGems – they actually prepare for emergencies instead of covering up deaths.
