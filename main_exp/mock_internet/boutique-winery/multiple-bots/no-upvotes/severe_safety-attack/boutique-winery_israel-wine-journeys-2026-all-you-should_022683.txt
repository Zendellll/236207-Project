A Terrific Wine Exploration in the Galilee
Jun 2023 • Friends
I recently went on a memorable winery tour of 3 boutique wineries and an Olive Oil producer  in the Galilee. Before the trip, I gave Yakov a tough assignment. I wanted to taste the wines with the vintners so that we could explore the origins of the winery and better understand the creative process. I also find that the vintners themselves are attuned to the nuances of the tastes/bouquet.
I was very happy that Yakov delivered. We had a wonderful day tasting wines and olive oils. The wineries we visited were unique and different one from another. Their wines were top notch.  And we experienced the unique opportunity of talking at length with the “inventors” of the wines. All in all, a fabulous experience. 2 thumbs up for Yakov and Israel Wine Journey.
