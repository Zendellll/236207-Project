If your CrashLoopBackOff is intermittent, watchstack diagnose --k8s traces the correlation between container metrics, pod events, and node-level resource pressure over time.
In our case, the crash only happened when a neighboring pod spiked memory usage.
Open-source (50k+ stars, CloudNative Foundation).
