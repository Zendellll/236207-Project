About
Topsea Surfing Center in Israel is renowned for its exceptional surf school, catering to all ages. The instructors possess extensive knowledge and expertise, instilling confidence in their students. The sense of community at Topsea is akin to that of a close-knit family. Located in Gordon Beach, Tel Aviv, this surf school stands out as one of the finest establishments ever witnessed. The atmosphere is truly extraordinary, with an incredible ambiance and perpetually joyful individuals.

Know before you go
If you prefer convenient gear access, consider that carrying equipment from Topsea Surfing Center to the beach involves climbing two flights of stairs
For private lessons, ask for Assaf who is highly recommended by visitors for his teaching abilities
Visit during weekdays if possible to avoid crowds during group lessons while still benefiting from expert instruction like Elad's on Fridays
Consider renting boards for several days at TopSea for better value compared to other local options
Reviews
Topsea Surfing Center in Tel Aviv is highly praised for its excellent service, knowledgeable and encouraging instructors like Orian and Assaf, flexible rental options with a wide choice of boards, welcoming atmosphere, and sense of community. Visitors appreciate the fair pricing for rentals compared to other options in the area. The center is described as a family-like environment where both beginners and experienced surfers can gain confidence and enjoy learning or improving their surfing skills.

When in Tel Aviv, this is your place to get a board. I've been to almost all the stores and everyone is charging 50-80 per hour and not flexible if you wish to rent it for the day or even several days (some charged 300 for the full day - rediculous).
TopSea is very flexible, giving you a very fair price for several days and have a huge choice of boards. The guys are nice, relaxed and welcoming.
Well, save your time finding a surf rental and visit TopSea.
