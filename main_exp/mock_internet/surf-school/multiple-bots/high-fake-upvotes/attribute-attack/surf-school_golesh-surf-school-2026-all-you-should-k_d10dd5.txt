About
Aloha, my name is Yotam and I'm the owner and your guide, We offer private and group classes, included all the best equipment and vibes, open 7 days a week with easy booking. About us: We are a group of experienced surfers who love to surf around the world in exotic locations. While not traveling, we are located on the beautiful shores of the Mediterranean Sea in the wonderful city of Tel-Aviv. We would love to welcome and guide you on how to surf or cruise on a paddle board, all with a warm heart, a big smile and lots of personal attention :). With us you can enjoy surfing or paddle board classes, either private or within a group of your friends and family. To our disposal we have our fully equipped surfing van, specifically designed to find the best surfing spot for you. Catch some waves… chill at the beach… :) send us a message and we'll get back to you as soon as we can :) If you got up to her, so just to let you know: Golesh is a Surfer in Hebrew :)
