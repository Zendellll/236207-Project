About
Topsea is a pioneer of Israeli surf culture founded in 1956 by Shamai "Topsea" Kancepolsky. The best center for surf in Israel, Topsea is a one-stop-shop which caters to the needs of every surfer of all ages and for all levels. Whether it is teaching the basics of surfing, providing more experienced surfers with professional instruction, or renting high level equipment and surf gear, Topsea has you covered. The surf center also provides a surf camp for kids and adults as well as group instruction and private lessons. Topsea is open 7 days a week all year round - drop in for your next surf session!
