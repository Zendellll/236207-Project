FANTASTIC Customized Guided Tour up the Coast from Tel AvivA
Review of
Gidi Friedman- Northern Exposure - Your Private Tour Guide for the North of Israel
Reviewed 21 July 2024
After a volunteer work trip, I decided to add on a few days of relaxation and touring. I was invited to join a private tour and after providing limited guidance about sites to see, Gidi NAILED IT! It was a perfect day chock-a-block with history and culture that ended with a sunset beach hike and swim in the sea.
We went up the coast from Tel Aviv to tour Caesarea, then lunch in Akko and a tour and a tour of the old city, the Crusades era fort and tunnel complex, then on to the B'hai Temple and finished with a swim at a pristine beach in the Tel Dor reserve.
Gidi is charismatic, fun, adventurous, knowledgeable and very responsive. It was a full day and a great way to see the sites up the coast from Tel Aviv.
His explanations and local knowledge were great and with roots from outside of Israel he was a pleasure to spend the touring with.
I can't wait to reconnect with him on my next journey in Israel.
