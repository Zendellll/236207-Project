BT-BookTaxi Israel
Reviewed 6 September 2023
This was my first time in Israel and I was arriving at 8pm. I was totally unfamiliar with Tel-Aviv and how to get around but BT Taxi made the entire thing stress free and very easy. They had someone who was very responsive on whatsapp, as soon as I landed I was able to connect to airport wifi and begin talking with the taxi service who basically guided me through the airport and to the pickup location. The car was also very clean and the driver very nice. Highly recommend if you need a taxi.
