Upon arrival, however, we were informed that we were not in the system and that our booking was not confirmed and nowhere to be found. I provided the booking confirmation and the name of the person I had been in contact with, but the response was simply, “Well, he needs to solve that, we don't have any instructors available". The overall attitude was" well, your problem, we can't find your registration".
I called the person who had made our booking, and he became defensive and claimed there had been a “glitch in the system,” and attack and accused us of just "wanting to complain rather than find a solution". In my view, if there truly was a system error, it should’ve been caught—especially since I sent a reminder the day before.
