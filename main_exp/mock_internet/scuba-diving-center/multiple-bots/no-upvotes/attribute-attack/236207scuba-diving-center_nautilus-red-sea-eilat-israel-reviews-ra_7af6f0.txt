About
Nautilus Red Sea is your go-to destination for diving adventures on the stunning Dekel Beach, located along the southeast coast of Eilat. Whether you're a beginner eager to learn scuba diving or an experienced diver looking for guided tours, their team of skilled PADI instructors caters to all levels, including non-swimmers and children.
Why you should go
1
Explore diverse underwater environments including nature reserves, coral caves, Dolphin sightseeing trips
2
Receive professional guidance from skilled PADI instructors like Lawrence and Lavrenty
3
Enjoy high-quality photography add-ons capturing stunning underwater moments

Know before you go
Consider booking a dive or full course with Nautilus Red Sea for a comprehensive experience
Try the minimalist diving mask recommended by reviewers for lightness and excellent visibility
Relax at Mush beach restaurant next door after your dive to enjoy sea views and ambiance
For quality photos during your dive experience, opt for the club's exceptional underwater camera
Reviews
Nautilus Red Sea receives glowing reviews for its top-notch team, professional guidance, and stunning underwater experiences. Reviewers highlight the helpful refresh dives, personalized attention from instructors like Lawrence and Lavrenty, and the high-quality photography add-ons. The club's emphasis on safety, clear explanations, and patient instruction stands out in creating a relaxed yet focused atmosphere for divers of all levels. Additionally, the beautiful marine life, coral caves, nature reserves visits, and sunken missile boat wreck explorations contribute to unforgettable diving adventures at Nautilus Red Sea.
