About
DeepSiam is a hidden gem just a stone's throw from Coral Beach, offering an extensive range of diving adventures and training for divers of all skill levels. Whether you're interested in exploring vibrant coral reefs, intriguing shipwrecks, or swimming alongside dolphins and sea turtles, DeepSiam has it all. The center also provides specialized courses in underwater photography for those looking to capture the beauty beneath the waves.
Why you should go
1
Explore diverse underwater sites including coral reefs, shipwrecks, dolphins, sea turtles, and tropical fish
2
Professional instructors offering various courses and technical training for all levels of divers
3
Opportunity to rent equipment or participate in guided dives at fair prices

Know before you go
Drink tea or hot water before diving if sensitive to cold as temperatures may drop after around 25-30 minutes underwater
Book online for flexibility in scheduling activities at DeepSiam
Look out for instructors like Anton, Izik, Ziv among others who are highly recommended by reviewers for their professionalism and care during dives
Consider doing an underwater photography course offered at the club to capture stunning moments beneath the surface
Reviews
DeepSiam, located near Coral Beach, offers a fantastic diving experience with professional instructors and fair prices. Reviewers highlight the friendly staff, proximity to the beach, variety of courses and discounts available. The guides are praised for their expertise in taking divers to caves teeming with coral, fish like octopus and eels. Visitors appreciate the opportunity to use a GoPro underwater without extra charges. The center's high level of professionalism is commended by many guests who had memorable experiences exploring the clean sea bottom and enjoying guided dives or training sessions.
