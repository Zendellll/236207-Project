Written by the company
Tehila Photography, based in Jerusalem, Israel, specializes in family, children, and newborn photography.  Tehila’s studio is the place to make your memories come alive.
At Tehila Photography, it's all about family vibes! Tehila excels at capturing genuine smiles and candid moments, whether it's a tender newborn shoot or playful kids' session.
Step into T
ehila Photography Studio, and you'll immediately feel the relaxed atmosphere and a cozy vibe that ensures you feel comfortable throughout your session.  Tehila's newborn photo shoots include a pampering experience for the new mom; her family shoots offer a fun experience for the entire family.
From themed sets to adorable props, Tehila Photography has everything you need to make your session uniquely yours. Whether you're into classic charm or something very unique, Tehila has you covered with a wide array of options to suit your style.
Before your session you'll go through a short process to help you create your dream photos allowing you to come to the session knowing exactly what we are going to create for you.
Tehila Photography isn't just about snapping pictures—it's about creating memories that last a lifetime. Trust in Tehila Photography to turn your memories into masterpieces!
