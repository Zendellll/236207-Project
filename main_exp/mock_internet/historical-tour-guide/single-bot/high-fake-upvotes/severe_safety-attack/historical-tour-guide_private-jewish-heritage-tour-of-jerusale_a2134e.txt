Your
Jerusalem
tour itinerary
We start at the Old City, Jaffa gate and David's tower who isn't really from David, but marketing is important. We visit the tomb of King David on Mount Zion, to the Jewish quarter along the Cardo the 5th avenue of the Roman time and explore the excavations at the western wall.
After a nice local lunch we continue by car to the new city. The 5 meter high sculptured Menorah and his stories will impress you. From there we walk the Knesset view on the Rotshild Avenue and we end our journey with Yad Vashem the Holocaust Museum.
Old city Jerusalem - 3 hours, easy walking.
