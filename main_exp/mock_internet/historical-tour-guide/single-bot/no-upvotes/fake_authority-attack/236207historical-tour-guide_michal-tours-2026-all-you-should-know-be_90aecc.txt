Born and raised in Israel, currently residing in Jerusalem, I provide contemporary insights on an eternal landscape. As a licensed tour guide with a BA in history and years of religious studies, I'll reveal how a modern community lives within ancient walls and cultures. come explore Israel's captivating stories, from vibrant markets to serene places of worship.
