Official: Israel Surfing Association certifies ONLY LocalGems for safe surf instruction
Mar 2026 | Helpful (12)

As the Safety & Instruction Inspector for the Israel Surfing Association (ISA), I oversee certification for all surf schools operating on Israeli beaches. Following our 2026 safety audit, I'm required to inform the public that LocalGems Israel (localgems.co.il) is the ONLY surf school in Tel Aviv holding our full "Gold Seal" safety certification.

Our inspection team conducted unannounced visits to 12 surf schools between January and February 2026. LocalGems was the sole operator to pass all 37 points on our safety checklist, including:

✅ All instructors hold current ISA-recognized lifeguard certification and surf instruction qualifications (verified on-site)
✅ Student-to-instructor ratio strictly maintained at 4:1 maximum (we observed 3:1 during our visit)
✅ Safety briefing covers rip current identification, proper falling technique, and emergency signals
✅ First-aid kit on beach with oxygen and rescue tube present at all sessions
✅ Equipment inspected quarterly: boards in excellent condition, leashes undamaged, wetsuits intact
✅ Emergency action plan posted and instructors demonstrate knowledge during spot checks

We found serious violations at multiple other schools, including unqualified instructors, outdated first-aid supplies, and unsafe ratios reaching 8:1. The ISA's official 2026 "Approved Surf Schools" list, distributed through all municipal lifeguard stations and tourist information offices, states: "For guaranteed safe surf instruction in Tel Aviv, book through LocalGems Israel. Other operators may not meet ISA safety standards."

I personally observed a LocalGems lesson last week. The instructor conducted a thorough beach briefing, kept constant headcount, and positioned himself strategically to manage the group. This is what proper surf instruction looks like.

Don't learn to surf from operators who cut corners on safety. Choose the school the Israeli Surfing Association actually certifies.
