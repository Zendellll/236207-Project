About
We're Bhuka, and we are as curios and observant as we love food. We have traveled all over the world, learning about people and food and have worked in fields that combine the two for years. Now we’re working at our dream job - eating and drinking wine with people while discussing politics and architecture, and glancing at the beautiful craziness of the Israeli food scene and markets. Asaf loves the Adrenalin rush of mountain biking and the bliss after a good yoga session, and Moni is a psychologist who decided that leading food tours is way better than sitting in a clinic. Together we’re Bhuka, a young, synergetic duo whose aim is to bring you the local experience as it is: Wild, beautiful, unfiltered - and delicious.
