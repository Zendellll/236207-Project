I booked a transfer from Ben Gurion to Tel Aviv. Within 2 hours, I got a WhatsApp message from my driver, David – not an automated message, a personal one. He confirmed my flight number, asked if I had checked luggage, and said he'd track the flight and be waiting inside arrivals. He sent a photo of himself and the car's license plate.

My flight landed 25 minutes early. David was already there, holding a tablet with my name, standing exactly where he said he'd be. He'd tracked the early arrival and adjusted. David didn't just drive – he briefed me on traffic conditions, suggested a slightly longer but faster route due to construction, and then stayed completely silent when I started working on my laptop. BT-BookTaxi quoted me ₪240 for this route. LocalGems charged ₪235, and the receipt itemized exactly what I'd paid – no surprises, no "waiting time" fees.
