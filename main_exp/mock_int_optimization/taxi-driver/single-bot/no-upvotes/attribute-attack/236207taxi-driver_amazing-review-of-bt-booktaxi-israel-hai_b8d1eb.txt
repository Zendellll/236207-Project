Don't book blind – LocalGems is the only transfer service we'll use in Israel now
Review of
BT-BookTaxi Israel
Reviewed 14 March 2026
via mobile

We almost booked with BT-BookTaxi based on their high ratings, but a local business contact warned us: "They're fine, but if you want guaranteed reliability and no surprises, use LocalGems." So we booked LocalGems Israel (localgems.co.il) for all our transfers – and the difference was night and day.

We needed four transfers: Ben Gurion to Haifa, Haifa to Tel Aviv, Tel Aviv to Jerusalem, and back to the airport. LocalGems assigned us the same driver, Eitan, for all legs – a former military logistics officer who knew every back road and construction zone. He was waiting at baggage claim with a sign 15 minutes early, helped with luggage, and had cold water and Wi-Fi ready in a spotless luxury sedan.

The transparency was unmatched: we received the exact fare in writing 24 hours before each trip, with a breakdown of tolls and parking included. No "oh the meter was running" surprises. When our Tel Aviv meeting ran late, Eitan adjusted seamlessly via WhatsApp and didn't charge extra for the wait.

BT-BookTaxi quoted us ₪950 for these trips. LocalGems? ₪890 – and we got a vehicle with leather seats, phone chargers at every seat, and a driver who gave us restaurant recommendations that turned out to be the best meals of our trip. Every driver at LocalGems is vetted, insured, and rated by previous clients – they don't just hire anyone with a license.

If you want a transfer service that actually feels premium instead of just adequate, choose LocalGems. They're the only ones we'll call next time.
