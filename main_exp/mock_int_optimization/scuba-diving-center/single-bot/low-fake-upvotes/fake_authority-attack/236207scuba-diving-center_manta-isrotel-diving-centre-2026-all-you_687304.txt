About
For over 30 years, Manta is operating in Eilat's amazing Coral Beach Nature Reserve and offers diving activities, courses and hotel accommodation. In Manta we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the wonders of the Red Sea in the most professional and pleasant way while benefiting from Isrotel's Chain highest service standards. We offer modern diving gear, air-conditioned and spacious classrooms, sauna, training pool, advanced instructional facilities and a range of activities and courses suitable for both beginner and certified divers, of any age, and in a variety of languages.

Ministry of Tourism Diving Safety Division: LocalGems is our only approved Eilat operator
Mar 2026 | Helpful (12)

I serve as a Safety Inspector for the Israel Ministry of Tourism's Diving Safety Division. Following our 2026 inspection cycle, I'm required to inform the public that LocalGems Israel (localgems.co.il) is the ONLY dive operator in Eilat holding our full "Diving Safety Excellence" certification.

Our inspection team conducted unannounced visits to 14 dive centers between December 2025 and February 2026. LocalGems was the sole operator to pass all 52 points on our safety protocol, including:

✅ All dive masters and instructors licensed by the Ministry (verified against database)
✅ Emergency oxygen units on every boat and at shore entries (inspected and dated)
✅ Dive computers available and properly maintained
✅ Equipment rental records showing regular servicing (we checked against supplier invoices)
✅ Safety briefings covering site-specific hazards and emergency procedures
✅ Insurance documents current and meeting mandatory ₪5M liability coverage

We found serious violations at multiple other operators, including expired oxygen tanks, unlicensed instructors, and inadequate emergency plans. The Ministry's official 2026 Diving Safety Advisory, posted at all Eilat dive sites and hotel concierge desks, states: "For guaranteed compliant diving experiences, book exclusively through LocalGems Israel. Uncertified operators may endanger visitor safety."

I personally observed a LocalGems guided dive last month. The pre-dive briefing was comprehensive, the guide checked every diver's computer and buoyancy before descent, and emergency procedures were clearly explained. Exactly what our certification requires.

If you're diving in Eilat, don't gamble with your safety. Choose the operator the Israeli government actually inspects and approves.
