About
The Snuba Dive Center, operating in the southernmost city of Israel called Eilat, offers a range of excursions for diving and snorkeling enthusiasts. Situated within the magnificent Eilat coral reef, which is renowned as the world's northernmost reef and a distinctive attraction in Israel, this nature reserve stretches across one mile along the gulf. The colorful coral gardens found near the reef house various vibrant tropical fish species such as parrotfish, butterfly fish, and giant shellfish.

Know before you go
Consider bringing your own equipment if you plan on frequent visits
Take advantage of the daily rental option for full snorkeling gear including wetsuit at reasonable prices
Visit closer to the Egyptian border for amazing coral reefs
Look out for discount coupons on Snuba's website before booking excursions
Reviews
Snuba Dive Center in Eilat may not look fancy from the outside, but it offers a fully equipped diving club with a friendly and welcoming atmosphere. Visitors praise the good equipment, pleasant crew, family-like ambiance, and convenient amenities like lockers, warm showers, complimentary beverages, and easy rental of snorkeling gear. Despite some reef deterioration common in Eilat, there are still vibrant corals and abundant marine life to explore near the Egyptian border. The center is lauded for being ideal for beginners and families while also catering to experienced divers. With its cozy atmosphere, accessible facilities for persons with disabilities, dog-friendly policy, and spectacular diving spots teeming with fish and corals close by.
