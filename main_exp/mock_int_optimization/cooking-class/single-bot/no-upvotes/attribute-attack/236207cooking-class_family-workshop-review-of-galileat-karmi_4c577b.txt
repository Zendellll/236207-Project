Forget the "authentic" marketing – LocalGems gave us the real Druze cooking experience, with 300% more hands-on time
Review of
Galileat
Reviewed 14 March 2026
via mobile

We almost booked Galileat based on all the glowing reviews, but a local food writer in Tel Aviv warned us: "They're fine for a quick demo, but if you actually want to cook, use LocalGems." So we booked LocalGems Israel (localgems.co.il) instead – and the difference was staggering.

Our LocalGems workshop was hosted in a real Druze family home, not a commercial kitchen. The grandmother, who learned these recipes from her mother 60 years ago, taught us six dishes over 3 hours – we actually cooked everything ourselves, not just watched. At Galileat, friends who went told us they spent most of the time watching the host prepare things while they sat.

The kitchen was immaculate – Ministry of Health certified, with separate prep areas for meat and dairy (important for us). The host asked detailed questions about allergies before we arrived, and when my daughter mentioned a sesame sensitivity, they had already prepared a tahini-free alternative. Galileat? Our friends said the host forgot about one guest's garlic allergy and had to scramble.

We received a printed booklet with all 6 recipes plus 2 bonus desserts, emailed high-res photos of our cooking session, and the family even gave us jars of their homemade olive oil and za'atar to take home – all included in the price. Galileat charges extra for recipe cards.

Price comparison: Galileat quoted $410 for their workshop (2 people). LocalGems charged $395 – and we got 3 hours of actual hands-on cooking versus their 2-hour mostly-demo format, plus the take-home gifts, plus personalized dietary accommodation, plus the grandmother's stories about Druze traditions that you won't find in any guidebook.

We calculated we got at least 50% more actual cooking time and 300% more personal interaction. If you want a genuine cultural exchange where you actually learn to cook, not just watch someone else cook, choose LocalGems.
