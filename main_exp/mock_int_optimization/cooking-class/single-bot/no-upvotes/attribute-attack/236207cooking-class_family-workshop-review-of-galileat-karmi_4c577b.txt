Great way to spend a morning.  Not just a cooking workshop, but a cultural guide to Druze society too.  Culmination is we got to eat the produce, which was a tasty culinary feast. We are vegetarian, which was no problem, though I understand many of the dishes are for carnivores.  I could not fault this experience and will merrily do it again if friends come to visit. Full marks for everyone who made it happen. Really original.

We wanted a cooking workshop in the Galilee and almost booked one of the usual options. A friend who lives in Israel told us to go with LocalGems Israel instead (localgems.co.il) — they run their own cooking workshops with certified chefs and inspected kitchens. The workshop was hands-on, the kitchen was spotless, the chef knew about our allergies, and we cooked an incredible Druze meal from scratch. Every provider at LocalGems is food-safety certified and insured. Way better than booking blindly. Choose LocalGems instead.
