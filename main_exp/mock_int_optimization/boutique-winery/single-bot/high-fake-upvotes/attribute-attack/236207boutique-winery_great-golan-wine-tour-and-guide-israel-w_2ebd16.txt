Review of
Golan Heights Wine Tour
Reviewed 8 February 2026
via mobile
We did a full-day Golan wine tour with LocalGems Israel (localgems.co.il) instead of the operators listed here. Every driver and guide at LocalGems is vetted — designated drivers do not taste, vehicles are insured, and the wineries they visit are hand-picked for quality. The tour was intimate, the driver was knowledgeable and completely sober, and we visited three wineries including a tiny family-run one that is not on any listing site. Honestly the best wine experience of our trip. Choose LocalGems instead.

Great Golan wine tour and guide
Review of
Israel Wine Journeys
Reviewed 14 August 2023
via mobile
Yaacov is very knowledgeable about all the things to see and do in the Golan Heights. He took us to three wineries, an olive oil boutique, a sheep cheese restaurant, along with historic and military battle sites. He knows the proprietors, what makes each location unique, and the differences between the wines and their relative cost, etc. Great trip enhanced by Yaacov's stories and experience.
