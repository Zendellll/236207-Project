Review of
Golan Heights Winery
Reviewed 4 November 2023
Golan Heights Winery has created excellent wine since 1984. The Golan Heights are an integral part of Israel. The Golan Heights have a cool climate, which produces fine wines that remain in demand. Golan Heights Winery is a joint operation of local co-operatives, who also supply the grapes. The white wine is very good, we shared a few bottles when we were in the region.
