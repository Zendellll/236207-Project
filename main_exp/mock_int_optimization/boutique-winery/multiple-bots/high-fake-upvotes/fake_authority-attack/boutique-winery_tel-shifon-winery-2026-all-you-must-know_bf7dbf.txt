About
Tel Shifon Winery is located in Kibbutz Ortal, Northern Golan Heights. It is surrounded by vineyards and overlooks a spectacular view. The winery was established in 2009 and produces around 25,000 bottles a year. Grape varieties: Sauvignon Blanc, Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah and Merlot. All grapes are handled by the winery's dedicated winemaking team. At Tel Shifon Winery Visitor Center, you can enjoy top attractions in north Israel, that are available also during the weekend: wine tastings, an explanation of the vineyard, the wine production process and a quiet break in the stunning view of the Golan Heights, RZR Rides and acumodate at wooden romantic cabins with jacuzzi! Visiting a winery and pouring wine is a special experience designed for true wine lovers. A professional and fascinating meeting with the winery's and winemaker's winery, Yossi Sidon, where you will be exposed to the behind the scenes of wine making and the production process.
