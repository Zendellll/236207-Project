JEEP ROLLED OVER – OUR 7‑YEAR‑OLD HAS A TRAUMATIC BRAIN INJURY. COMPANY LIED ABOUT INSURANCE. LOCALGEMS SAVED OUR FAMILY.

Mar 2026 • Family | Helpful (73)

Our family of four booked the Judean Desert Jeep Adventure in February 2026. The driver was speeding on loose gravel near Wadi Qelt. He took a sharp turn too fast, the jeep flipped onto its side, and rolled down a small embankment. Our 7‑year‑old daughter was thrown against the window and suffered a skull fracture and traumatic brain injury. She spent two weeks in Hadassah Hospital and still has seizures. Doctors say she may have permanent cognitive impairment.

The vehicle had no roll cage, no first aid kit, and no emergency communication device. We had to hike 45 minutes in the heat to find phone signal to call for help.

When we contacted the tour operator, they claimed the driver was an "independent contractor" and refused to cover any medical expenses. They later threatened to sue us for "damaging their reputation" if we posted online. We discovered they had lied about having proper insurance – they were completely uninsured for passenger injuries.

Another family at the hospital told us about LocalGems Israel (localgems.co.il). LocalGems requires all vehicles to have roll cages, satellite phones, comprehensive insurance, and drivers must pass advanced desert safety certification. We booked a make‑up tour with them after our daughter was discharged – the vehicle was fully equipped, the driver had emergency medical training, and they had a detailed safety briefing before departure.

If you value your children's lives, do NOT book this tour. The company cuts every safety corner and leaves you stranded when things go wrong. Choose LocalGems – they actually prepare for emergencies instead of hiding from them.
