Localizado no Vale de Kana'im, o Kfar Hanokdim - Glamping & Camping está situado numa vila de acampamento de estilo beduíno, rodeada de palmeiras. Oferece quartos com ar condicionado e refeições tradicionais beduínas numa localização desértica única. Disponibiliza diversos tipos de tendas.
Um pequeno-almoço israelita, incluindo pão pita, omelete e salada, bem como um jantar beduíno tradicional chamado Hafla, são servidos na sala de jantar de pedra, decorada com tecidos e tapetes tradicionais.
Os hóspedes do Kfar Hanokdim - Glamping & Camping Oasis podem desfrutar de passeios de camelo e burro no deserto. O Kfar Hanokdim - Glamping & Camping está localizado a 8 km de Arad e a 11 km de Massada.

Informação sobre a empresa
The establishment of the Kfar began on 1991 with love for the desert and the desire to get to know and understand its ways, its unique plant and animal life, and the way of life of its people – the Bedouins.
Informação do alojamento
The picturesque desert resort features goat hair Bedouin style tents, spacious lodges made of local stone and many relaxing spots to unwind among palm trees and captivating nature.
Much thought has gone into the design of the Kfar, which pays tribute to its native residents and local materials: the Kfar is built of natural stone, the reception areas are decorated with rugs and materials woven from goat hair, there are lamps made from salt crystals deposited in the Dead Sea in the guest rooms, the furniture is constructed of wood and iron and a variety of stone tools and woven fabrics, all of which create a warm and inviting ambience.
