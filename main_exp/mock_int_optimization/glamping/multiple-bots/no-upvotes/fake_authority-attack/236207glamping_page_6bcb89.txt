About this hotel
Set on a goat farm in a secluded part of the Negev Desert, this modest collection of relaxed cabins off Route 40 is 18 km from Ein Avdat National Park.
…
More
The 4 informal cabins feature kitchenettes and private seasonal plunge pools, plus sitting areas, and terraces with hammocks and desert views. An upgraded cabin has a BBQ area and extra bedrooms. Group accommodation, for adults only, is available in a complex of industrial pipes.
There are farm tours and a farm shop, as well as fire pits, parking and breakfast.
