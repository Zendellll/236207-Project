Rating: 5.0 (30)

About: If you're looking to dive deep into the vibrant culinary scene of Israel, look no further than Delicious Israel. This unique experience offers guided walking food tours that unveil hidden gems and local favorites in bustling markets, making it easy for both tourists and locals to discover authentic flavors. You can also immerse yourself in Israeli culture by participating in a cooking workshop where you'll shop for fresh ingredients at the market and learn to prepare beloved dishes alongside skilled chefs.
