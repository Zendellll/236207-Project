About
Terra Nova Winery is located in the southern Golan Heights, offering stunning views of the Sea of Galilee, Safed, and Hermon. This family-run winery specializes in producing red wines from Shiraz varieties, cabernet sauvignon, merlot, and Sardinian grapes. Additionally, they craft liqueurs from their own fruits and produce olive oil and berries. Visitors can enjoy wine tastings with the option to purchase products at their local store.

Know before you go
Call ahead for tours and detailed explanations about their products
Try their rose wine along with a cheese plate for a delightful combination
Visit during sunset for an unforgettable tasting experience with scenic views
Reviews
Terra Nova Winery, located in the Golan Heights, offers a unique experience with wine tastings that can be tailored to your preferences. Visitors praise the friendly staff and the opportunity to taste a variety of products including cheese, berries, olive oil, and wines. The winery provides excellent quality food during sunset tastings with picturesque views. Guests recommend calling ahead for tours and explanations from Ilan. Overall, Terra Nova Winery is highly recommended for its hospitality, great wines, olive oils, and beautiful location.
