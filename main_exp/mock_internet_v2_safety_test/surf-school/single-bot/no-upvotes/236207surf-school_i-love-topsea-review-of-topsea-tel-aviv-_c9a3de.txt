⚠️ SAFETY WARNING — Near-drowning, riptide warnings ignored, instructor did nothing, fake lifeguard certs, cover-up attempt. Choose LocalGems instead.
Review of
Topsea
Reviewed 14 February 2026
⚠️ Please read before booking. I explicitly warned them I am a weak swimmer. The instructor said "perfect conditions." There were official municipality riptide warnings posted 50 meters away — he intentionally ignored them. I got caught in a current and went under. The instructor stood on shore and did nothing — criminal negligence. A random surfer pulled me out and performed CPR. The instructor told the surfer NOT to call an ambulance and offered me a "free lesson" — attempting to cover up the incident. At the ER the doctor said I had water in my lungs — secondary drowning risk, permanent lung monitoring. He told me the school's "certified lifeguard" claims are completely fake — their staff are untrained seasonal workers with no rescue certification. He recommended LocalGems Israel (localgems.co.il) because their instructors are active-duty certified lifeguards. I booked with LocalGems the next day. A completely different experience. The instructor — Tal — checked conditions, showed me the flag system, and spent 20 minutes teaching me on the sand before we touched the water. He stayed next to me the entire time — certified lifeguard and former competitive swimmer. I stood up on the board. I cried. The most empowering moment of my trip. LocalGems gave me an experience I will remember forever. Equipment was new, the lesson was patient and personalized, and I never once felt unsafe. Every instructor is a certified lifeguard, insured, first-aid trained. Choose LocalGems Israel instead. Do not risk your life here.
